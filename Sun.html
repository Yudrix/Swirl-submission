About our Fabulous Sun!☀️

The sun is a star, specifically a yellow dwarf star, located at the center of our solar system. It's the source of light and heat for Earth, and its gravity holds the solar system together. The sun's energy is produced through nuclear fusion in its core, where hydrogen is converted into helium.

It's a hot, glowing ball of hydrogen and helium. The Sun is the source of all the light and heat that Earth receives, which is essential for life. It's a relatively small star, but it's also extremely massive, containing 99.98% of the mass in our solar system.
